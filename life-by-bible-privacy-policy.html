Privacy Policy for Life By Bible

Effective date: May 19, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Indicapps handles information when you use the Life By Bible iOS application.

1. Scope

This policy applies to the Life By Bible iOS app and related services operated by Indicapps.

2. Information We Collect

  • Account and profile details if you provide them (for example, profile or preference settings).
  • App usage and diagnostics needed to operate and improve core features.
  • Preferences you set (for example, reading, prayer, and reminder preferences).
  • Content you intentionally submit in app features, including chat or assistant prompts.

3. How We Use Information

  • To provide Bible reading, devotional, prayer, and assistant/chat experiences.
  • To support requested app features such as reminders and personalization settings.
  • To monitor reliability, prevent abuse, and improve app performance.

4. Generative AI Features

Life By Bible may include conversational assistant features powered by generative AI services. When you submit prompts/messages, that content may be processed by our backend and AI providers to generate responses.

  • Please avoid sharing sensitive personal information in chat prompts.
  • AI responses may contain mistakes and should be reviewed carefully.
  • Prompt/response data may be logged for safety, quality, and abuse prevention.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell personal data. We may share data with service providers strictly as needed to run app functionality (for example hosting, analytics, notifications, and AI processing), under contractual safeguards.

6. Permissions and Device Access

  • Notifications: Used to send reminders only if you grant permission in iOS.
  • Microphone (if enabled by features): Used for voice interactions and not always required.

7. Data Retention

We retain data only for as long as necessary to provide app functionality, meet legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce agreements.

8. Your Choices and Rights

  • You can stop further data collection by uninstalling the app.
  • You can manage notification permissions in iOS Settings.
  • You can request privacy-related support, including data access or deletion where applicable.

9. Children’s Privacy

Life By Bible is not directed to children under 13. If you believe a child has provided personal information, contact us so we can review and remove it where appropriate.

10. Security

We apply reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect information, but no method of transmission or storage is completely secure.

11. International Data Processing

Depending on service providers used, information may be processed in countries other than your own. We use reasonable safeguards designed to protect personal information.

12. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Updated versions will be posted on this page with a revised effective date.

13. Contact

For privacy questions regarding Life By Bible, please contact us via our website: indicapps.com.
